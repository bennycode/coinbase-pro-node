### connect

▸ **connect**(`reconnectOptions?`): `default`

The websocket feed is publicly available, but connections to it are rate-limited to 1 per 4 seconds per IP.

**`see`** https://docs.pro.coinbase.com/#websocket-feed

#### Parameters

| :-- | :-- | :-- |
